Subsidies Opportunities

Keeping you up-to-date with the current state of affairs.



 

















Subsidy Category Description
Employment



Location: Johor
Time: March 2023 		The Johor state government initiates an employment subsidy program in March 2023 to address unemployment in the agricultural sector. Recognizing the importance of agriculture to Johor's economy, the program offers financial incentives and tax breaks to companies that hire and train local residents for agricultural roles. By focusing on industries such as crop cultivation, livestock farming, and agro-processing, the subsidy aims to create job opportunities, strengthen the agricultural workforce, and promote sustainable agricultural practices in Johor Bahru.

Import



Location: Kuala Lumpur
Time: July 2022 		In July 2022, the Malaysian government introduces an import subsidy program in Kuala Lumpur tailored to support the agricultural sector. Importers of essential agricultural inputs such as seeds, fertilizers, and agricultural machinery receive financial assistance and tax incentives to reduce import costs. This initiative aims to enhance the competitiveness of local farmers, improve agricultural productivity, and promote self-sufficiency in food production by ensuring affordable access to quality agricultural inputs.

Export



Location: Melaka
Time: November 2024 		The Melaka state government launches an export subsidy program in November 2024 aimed at boosting agricultural exports. Recognizing the potential of Melaka's agricultural products in international markets, exporters of commodities such as palm oil, fruits, and spices receive grants, marketing assistance, and logistical support to expand their export activities. By promoting the export of high-quality agricultural products, the subsidy program aims to increase foreign exchange earnings, drive economic growth, and enhance the reputation of Melaka as a key player in the global agricultural trade.

Tax



Location: Penang
Time: February 2025 		In February 2025, the Penang state government introduces a tax subsidy program targeted at stimulating investment in agricultural innovation. Recognizing the importance of technological advancements in agriculture, companies investing in research and development, agri-tech startups, and sustainable farming practices in Penang enjoy reduced corporate tax rates and tax exemptions. By incentivizing investment in high-tech agricultural sectors such as precision farming, biotechnology, and agricultural robotics, the subsidy program aims to drive innovation, improve productivity, and ensure the sustainability of Penang's agriculture industry.

Production



Location: Sabah
Time: September 2023 		The Sabah state government implements a production subsidy program in September 2023 to bolster the agricultural sector. Recognizing the diverse agricultural potential of Sabah, the subsidy program provides grants, technical assistance, and infrastructure support to manufacturers and farmers in sectors such as palm oil processing, aquaculture, and eco-tourism. By promoting value-added agricultural activities, diversifying products, and adopting sustainable practices, the subsidy aims to enhance productivity, create employment opportunities, and foster inclusive rural development in Kota Kinabalu and surrounding regions.
